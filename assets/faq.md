## Check-in

- Each guest receives an Access Code (PIN), a Room Number, and a Bed Number.
- These details are delivered separately by the hostel staff before arrival.
- If the guest has not received their check-in details, advise them to contact
  the admin phone.

## How to open the doors

- The main entrance and each room door have a black keypad with numbers.
- Type the Access Code on the keypad, then press the Checkmark (✔) to confirm.
- Example: if the code is 1234, type "1234" then ✔.
- The code stops working at 11:00 AM on the departure day.

## Bathrooms & showers

- All bathrooms and showers are on the lower floor.
- There are separate sections for Men and Women — please use the correct one.

## Kitchen & food

- Put all dirty dishes in the dishwasher.
- Use the kitchen stickers to write your Name and Departure Date on your food.
- Food without a label will be removed.

## Quiet hours & phones

- Quiet time after 23:00 (11:00 PM).
- No speakers in the rooms — use headphones for music, videos, and calls.
- Do not make phone calls while in bed.

## Not allowed

- No smoking inside the building.
- No alcohol anywhere inside the hostel.
- Breaking the rules may result in immediate eviction.

## Check-out

- Check-out is before 11:00 AM.
- Late check-out is charged an extra fee.
